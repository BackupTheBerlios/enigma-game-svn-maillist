+<a name="s6q4">
+<h4>Why are the Enigma services located at so many different locations?
+What is located at Berlios, what at nongnu, what at enigma-game.org etc?</h4></a>
+<p>When Enigma got public the services of Savannah (nongnu.org) seemed
+appropiated. As time went on the development switched between different
+source code repositories like Archie and CVS. Finally after release of
+Enigma 0.92 we were looking for a provider who offered Subversion repository support.
+At that point of time Berlios was one of the few that offered the required
+services. Thus you will find the post 0.92 development on Berlios and older
+repositories at Savannah.</p>
+<p>Other services like the Forum and the Wiki started and are still running
+as private services supplied by Enigma users at their addresses.</p>
+<p>The homepage is still served by Savannah. But this limits us to static
+contents. Thus we reserved the domain enigma-game.org, which may be used
+in future for dynamic services like score uploading, a network game server and
+more. Currently the address is redirected to Savannah and we are just using
+email services at this address.</p>
